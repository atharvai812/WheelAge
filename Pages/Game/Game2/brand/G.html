Gearbox

A gearbox, also known as a transmission, is a mechanical component in a vehicle that transfers power from the engine to the wheels. It allows the engine to operate at an optimal speed range while providing different gear ratios to match various driving conditions.

Function: The primary function of a gearbox is to convert the rotational power generated by the engine into a usable form for the wheels. It allows the engine to operate within its ideal speed range while providing torque multiplication and speed reduction as necessary.

Types of Gearbox: Gearbox ar mainly of two types Automatic Transmission ,and Manual Transmission nowadays, New technologies like Intelligent Manual transmission and DSG Gearbox have also been part of the market.

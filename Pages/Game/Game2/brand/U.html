UnderCarriege

Undercarriage is the part of a moving vehicle that is underneath the main body of the vehicle.

The undercarriage of a vehicle is responsible for supporting the weight of the vehicle, providing a stable platform for the wheels, and allowing the vehicle to move smoothly over uneven surfaces.

The undercarriage of a vehicle is typically made up of a number of components, including: The frame, The axles, The suspension, The brake, The exhaust system.

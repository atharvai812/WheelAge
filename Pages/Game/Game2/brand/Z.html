Z-Bar

A Z bar is a type of suspension component that is used to connect the front suspension of a car to the chassis. It is a long, thin bar that is typically made of steel or aluminum. The Z bar helps to keep the front suspension from twisting, which can improve handling and stability.

some of the benefits of using a Z bar: Improved handling: Z bars can help to improve handling by keeping the front suspension from twisting. This can make the car more responsive to steering inputs and can help to prevent the car from understeering or oversteering. Increased stability: Z bars can also help to increase stability by preventing the front suspension from twisting. This can make the car more stable in high-speed corners and can help to prevent the car from rolling over. Reduced body roll: Z bars can also help to reduce body roll by keeping the front suspension from twisting. This can make the car feel more planted on the road and can help to improve the driver's confidence.

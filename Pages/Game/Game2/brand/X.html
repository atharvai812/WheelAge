Xenon Light

Xenon lights, also known as high-intensity discharge (HID) lights or xenon headlights, are a type of automotive lighting technology that provides bright and intense illumination.

Technology: Xenon lights use a different technology compared to traditional halogen lights. Instead of a filament, xenon lights utilize an electric arc to produce light. The arc is created between two electrodes inside a xenon gas-filled bulb.

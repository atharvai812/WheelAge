Quick-Release Stearing Wheel

A quick-release steering wheel is a specialized steering wheel mechanism that allows for easy and rapid removal or installation of the steering wheel from the steering column. It is commonly used in motorsports, performance vehicles, and specialized applications.

Function: The primary function of a quick-release steering wheel is to provide a convenient method for removing the steering wheel from the steering column. This allows for easier entry and exit from the vehicle, improved access to the driver's seat, and added security by deterring theft.

Types of Quick-Release Mechanisms: There are different types of quick-release mechanisms available, including a. Push-button Release b. Twist-Release

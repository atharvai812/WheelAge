Navigation System

A car navigation system, also known as a GPS (Global Positioning System) navigation system, is a device or software application that provides navigation and route guidance for drivers. It uses satellite signals to determine the vehicle's location and offers turn-by-turn directions to reach a desired destination.

GPS Technology: Car navigation systems rely on the GPS technology to determine the vehicle's precise location using signals received from satellites orbiting the Earth. This information is then used to calculate the optimal route to the destination..

Maps and Routing: Car navigation systems typically come pre-loaded with maps, or they can access maps through an internet connection.Guiding drivers along the most efficient route to their destination. The routing can take into account factors like traffic conditions, road closures, and detours.

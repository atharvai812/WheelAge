Spark Plugs

A spark plug is an essential component of the ignition system in an internal combustion engine. It generates the electrical spark necessary to ignite the air-fuel mixture in the engine's combustion chamber.

The primary function of a spark plug is to produce an electrical spark that ignites the air-fuel mixture in the combustion chamber. This ignition initiates the combustion process, allowing the engine to generate power.

Types of Spark Plugs: There are different types of spark plugs available are Copper spark plugs,Iridium Spark Plugs,Platinum Spark Plugs

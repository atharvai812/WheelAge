Headlights

Headlights are the primary lighting units located at the front of a vehicle. They serve the purpose of providing illumination for the driver's visibility at night or in low-light conditions.

Function: The main function of headlights is to illuminate the road ahead and improve visibility for the driver, especially during nighttime driving or in adverse weather conditions such as fog, rain, or snow.

Types of Headlights: There are various types of headlights used in modern vehicles, including halogen headlights, xenon (HID) headlights, and LED headlights. Each type utilizes different lighting technology to generate and emit light.

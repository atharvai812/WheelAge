W Motors

W Motors is a luxury sports car manufacturer based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Founded in 2012, W Motors has gained recognition for producing exclusive, high-performance vehicles that combine cutting-edge technology with unique design aesthetics.

The company gained international fame with the launch of its first production car, the Lykan HyperSport, which gained prominence for its striking appearance and featured in the "Furious 7" film. The Lykan HyperSport boasted impressive performance figures, including a top speed of 240 mph (386 km/h) and acceleration from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 97 km/h) in under 2.8 seconds.

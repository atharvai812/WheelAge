Ultima

Ultima is a British sports car manufacturer known for producing high-performance, hand-built vehicles. Established in 1992, Ultima Sports Ltd. has become synonymous with extreme performance and exhilarating driving experiences.

Ultima specializes in creating lightweight, track-focused sports cars that offer blistering acceleration and exceptional handling. Their flagship model, is a supercar capable of reaching incredible speeds and delivering adrenaline-pumping performance on both the road and the racetrack.

Ultima cars are often powered by powerful engines, including V8 and V12 options, with impressive horsepower figures. The lightweight construction and aerodynamic design of their vehicles contribute to their remarkable performance capabilities.

Dodge

Dodge is a renowned American automotive brand known for its powerful and performance-oriented vehicles. Established in 1914, Dodge has a rich history of manufacturing cars, SUVs, and trucks that cater to enthusiasts seeking thrilling experiences on the road.

From the iconic Dodge Challenger and Charger muscle cars to the versatile Dodge Durango SUV and rugged Ram trucks, Dodge offers a diverse lineup that combines bold design with exhilarating performance.

As a brand with a strong presence in the American automotive market, Dodge continues to uphold its reputation for producing vehicles that exude power, style, and driving thrills.

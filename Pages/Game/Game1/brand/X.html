Xpeng

Xpeng Motors, also known as Xiaopeng Motors, is a Chinese electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer founded in 2014. The company specializes in the development and production of smart electric vehicles with a focus on innovation, technology, and connectivity.

Xpeng offers a range of all-electric vehicles that cater to different segments of the market. Their lineup includes models like the Xpeng P7, a high-performance electric sedan, and the Xpeng G3, a compact electric SUV. These vehicles are designed to provide an enjoyable and eco-friendly driving experience.

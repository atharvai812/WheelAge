Zotye

Zotye Auto is a Chinese automobile manufacturer that was founded in 2005. The company specializes in the production of affordable passenger vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, and electric
vehicles. Zotye has gained prominence in the Chinese market for its focus on providing cost-effective transportation solutions without compromising on quality. The brand offers a range of vehicles that cater to the needs of different customer segments, emphasizing practicality, reliability, and affordability.
In recent years, Zotye has made significant strides in the field of electric vehicles (EVs). They have developed a lineup of electric models that aim to contribute to the growing demand for sustainable mobility. Zotye EVs are designed to offer efficient and eco-friendly transportation options, with features like extended driving ranges and fast charging capabilities.

Ford

Ford is an iconic American automotive brand with a rich history and a global presence. Established in 1903 by Henry Ford, the company revolutionized the automobile industry with the introduction of the assembly line and the Model T, making cars more affordable and accessible to the masses.

Ford offers a diverse range of vehicles, including cars, trucks, SUVs, and electric models. Known for their durability, reliability, and innovation, Ford vehicles have become synonymous with quality craftsmanship and performance.

The Ford lineup includes popular models such as the Ford Mustang, known for its iconic design and exhilarating performance, and the Ford F-150, a best-selling pickup truck renowned for its capability and versatility.

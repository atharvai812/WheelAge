Qoros

Qoros is a relatively new automotive brand that was established in 2007 as a joint venture between Chinese automaker Chery Automobile and Israel Corporation. The company's mission is to produce high-quality vehicles that combine European design, advanced technology, and a focus on safety.

Qoros offers a range of compact and mid-size sedans, including models like the Qoros 3 and Qoros 5. These vehicles are designed to provide a comfortable and refined driving experience with a balance of performance.

Qoros places a strong emphasis on safety, incorporating advanced safety features and technologies into their vehicles. They have achieved high ratings in crash tests and received recognition for their commitment to safety standards.
